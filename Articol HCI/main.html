3. Project

  • Title

    Playlist Saver

  • Description

    In context of youtube deleting the videos or put videos in private I created an app of purpose to save the videos titles from your playlist. That would help people that saved music videos that got deleted due the copyright claim or the channel associated got deleted entirely. That leaves with only unavailable videos in your playlist. Also it would help you remind what videos got deleted in your playlist since everytime a video got deleted or was privated by the user it does not show the title anymore.

4. How to use

  • Login

    You need to sync your youtube account to the app. You need to make an account using google api.

  • Home

    You will have an option to synchronize your playlist. Also the option to stop this. Once you choose the option your playlist will be checked every day. It will save what videos got deleted on your playlist. You can also choose what playlist you want to be saved. "Liked Videos" is choosed by default.

  • Logs

    In this section the user can check the notification of the videos saved recently. You will also recieve an email with these notifications.
